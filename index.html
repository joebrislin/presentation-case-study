Background

Custom Web Application developed for Agency that specializes in mental health or substance abuse disorders

Where They Were

  • Client was unhappy with flow of current platform
  • They had to make concessions to make their process fit the software
  • Felt they were paying for features that they didn't use
  • Not much control over when bug fixes would be implemented

Where They Wanted To Be

  • Wanted to develop an application to fit their process
  • Needed application to grow as the business grew
  • Wanted to cut costs
  • Control of which features would be implemented

Architecture Project

Application Development

Hosting

  • Self Hosted (Cloud / Dedicated)
  • Managed Hosting
  • SAAS Platform

Pros

  • Client saw cost savings of over at 25% in 1st Year
  • No License Fees
  • Complete Control over Application
  • Customer Owns the Application (Proprietary)
  • Can continue to develop in-house if desired

Cons

  • Longer time to site launch
  • Much larger involvement from customer perspective - Time Consuming
  • Risk of not conveying requirements properly
  • No User Community for Support
